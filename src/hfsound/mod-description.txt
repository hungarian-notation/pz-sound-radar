<TEXT> Adds an HUD that displays the directions sounds are coming from.

This mod is designed primarily as an accessibility aid, as the game is balanced 
with an assumption that players possess normative binaural hearing. While the 
intent is not to make the game easier per se, it will enhance the player's 
situational awareness.

Note that the effects of the deaf, hard-of-hearing, and keen-hearing traits are 
modeled by this mod. This mod has no effect for characters with the deaf trait,
and characters with the other traits will have the range at which they perceive 
sounds altered accordingly.

This mod was inspired in part by the currently defunct `Visual Sounds` mod, 
though it has some major differences that I consider to be strict upgrades.
